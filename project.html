HomeAgain: A Pet Away from Home

Through HomeAgain, the users can petify an animal of their choice from any registered NGO and take up all the expenses of their pet's care take. They can witness the NGO take care of your chosen pet through the portal, monitor your chosen pet through weekly updates of photos and videos and there is also a posts section where users can share stories about animals who need help and attention. The project is still a work in progress.
